About Clownfish

Clownfish, also known as anemonefish, are a type of marine fish found in warm waters.

Habitat

Clownfish are commonly found in the shallow waters of the Indian and Pacific Oceans.

Diet

Clownfish primarily eat algae, zooplankton, worms, and small crustaceans.
